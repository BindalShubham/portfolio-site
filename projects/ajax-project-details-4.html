Medicare Bot

Patient Assistant
General Info

Helps booking an appointment with the doctor.

Project Details

Booking an appointment with the doctor is a tedious work to be manually handled when there are multiple doctors in multiple departments. Thus Medicare Virtual Assistant helps keeping track of all the appointments and viewing profile summary in your browser/skype.

Results

53% people check their reports on VA and 20% of reoccuring patients book their appointment through bot.
